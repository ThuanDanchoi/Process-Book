Introduction

Welcome to our website where we explore the intricate connections between diet, obesity, and life expectancy through innovative data visualizations. Utilizing dot plots and maps, our visual tools highlight the effects of dietary choices on public health across different countries. Our dot plots compare food consumption with obesity rates, while our maps illustrate the geographic distribution of obesity and its impact on life expectancy. Dive into our processed and curated data to discover how global dietary patterns influence health outcomes, providing valuable insights for policymakers, researchers, and the public interested in nutrition and health.
